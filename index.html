Rules

Bronzeman Mode

Wilderness Bronzeman is a PvP-first OSRS challenge where progress comes from Wilderness content, player kills, and milestone unlocks.

Wilderness Locked

Progress stays in the Wilderness and PvP spaces, including LMS and other PvP minigames.

PK Loot Unlocks

PK loot unlocks regular collection items, but anything that exists as a talent still requires buying that talent before use.

Tasks Buy Talents

Completed tasks award talent points for account power.

Kills Buy Supplies

Each player kill awards PK points that can be spent on GE shop purchases and repeatable KC grinds.

GE Purchases Use Shop

The shop is for items bought from the Grand Exchange instead of earned through Wilderness content.

Honor The Spirit

Off-meta, risky, and funny PK setups are encouraged.
